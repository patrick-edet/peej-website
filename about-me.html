Designing with Empathy, Coding with Clarity

I'm Patrick Edet Effiong Jay (PEEJ), a Learning Design Strategist, UX/UI Designer, and Junior Fullstack developer based in Budapest with 8+ years of experience turning complex problems into intuitive, user-centered solutions. I bring a multidisciplinary edge to my work, combining technical development skills with a deep understanding of how people learn and interact with digital systems. I've worked with startups, agencies, and enterprise clients to design digital products that feel effortless to use.

With a diploma in Fullstack Software Development from McMaster University and hands-on experience at global organizations like British Petroleum and Avis Budget Group, I specialize in designing user-focused learning platforms and applications that are both functional and engaging. My strengths lie in bridging design thinking with clean code, using tools like Figma, HTML, CSS design frameworks like Bootstrap or Tailwind, JavaScript Frameworks, Typescript, PostgreSQL, setting up database like MongoDB and Docker to create seamless digital experiences.

My background in learning design enables me to build smarter content delivery systems using LMS tools like Cornerstone, Workday, Degreed and Xyleme ensuring accessibility, performance, and impact at scale. I've led cross-functional projects, built high-impact learning catalogs, and received multiple awards for stakeholder onboarding and content innovation.

Whether I'm prototyping an intuitive interface or building a responsive backend, my focus is always on solving real-world problems with clarity and creativity. I'm driven by curiosity, collaboration, and a desire to design learning and tech solutions that truly serve people.

Let's build something meaningful — together.

Work Ethics & Core Values

  • Reliability - in the quality of my work and the consistency of my contributions
